## AI Policy
- Shouldn't we allow auto-generated commit messages?
- They are often imprecise or wrong
- In many cases they are better than e.g. "WIP1" → "WIP2" → "doesn't work" → "fix"
- However, writing proper commit messages is not that complicated if you have clear guidelines and make well-sized commits
- Not allowing auto-generated commit messages sounds reasonable for now
- If the draft is accepted, it will be translated to English and published on various channels
